Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 9, 2026

1. Our No-Logs Commitment

At Toogle VPN, we operate under a strict No-Logs Policy. Our systems are engineered to ensure that we do not store your private data. Because we do not collect this information, we cannot be compelled to provide data that does not exist.

What we NEVER collect:

  • Your IP address (source or VPN).
  • Your browsing history or destination websites.
  • The content of your data or DNS queries.
  • Connection timestamps or session durations.

2. Data We Do Collect

To provide our service, we collect the bare minimum amount of information required for account management:

  • Email Address: Used for account login and password recovery.
  • Payment Data: Processed via third-party providers. We do not store your full credit card details on our servers.
  • Aggregated Stats: We monitor total server load to ensure performance, but this is never tied to an individual user.

3. RAM-Only Infrastructure

Toogle VPN utilizes RAM-only servers. Unlike traditional servers that write to hard drives, our stack runs entirely in volatile memory. If a server is powered down or seized, all session data is instantly wiped and unrecoverable.

4. Jurisdictional Protection

Toogle VPN is incorporated in Switzerland. This allows our users to benefit from some of the strongest privacy laws in the world, remaining outside the jurisdiction of "14 Eyes" surveillance alliances.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. If we make significant changes that affect your rights, we will notify you via the email address associated with your account.

The TL;DR Version

Zero Logs: We don't know who you are or what you do online.

Diskless Servers: Data exists only in RAM and disappears on reboot.

Swiss Laws: Protected by the world's best privacy regulations.

You Own Your Data: We never sell or trade your email address.
